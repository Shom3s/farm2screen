# farm2screen

1.0 Project Overview

The Farm2screen is an innovative online marketplace designed to transform how farmers market and sell their pineapples directly to consumers, wholesalers, and businesses. Aligning with the project’s vision to transform the way people connect, shop and grow businesses online, the platform provides a convenient, trustworthy, and personalized e-commerce experience for both farmers and buyers.

At its core, the platform empowers pineapple farmers by giving them the tools to list, promote, and manage their products online without relying on middlemen. The platform aims to support real-time pricing, streamline order management, and provide broader market exposure through increased online visibility and enable farmers to expand their customer base and boost sales.

2.0 App Features

1. Daily Sales Summary Pop-Up (Entrepreneur)

   This pop-up screen displays a daily sales summary for the entrepreneur. It highlights key information such as the number of orders received and total sales value for the day. This feature provides quick insights into daily business performance without navigating to the full analytics page.


2.  “My Products” Screen (Entrepreneur)

     This screen displays the product management module for entrepreneurs. It allows users to view all registered products along with their prices, stock quantities, and availability status. Search and filter options are provided to help entrepreneurs manage products efficiently. The “Add New Product” button enables users to add new products to the system.


3. Edit Product Screen (Entrepreneur)

    This screen allows entrepreneurs to update existing product information. Users can edit the product image, product name, category, price, unit of measurement, stock quantity, and product description. The editable form ensures that product details remain accurate and up to date. Once changes are completed, the “Update Product” button saves the updated information into the system.


4. “Announcements & Technical Information” Screen (Entrepreneur)

    This screen presents announcements and technical information related to pineapple sales and farming activities. Each announcement includes a title, brief description, and validity period. This module helps entrepreneurs stay updated with important information, promotions, and general notices relevant to their business.


5. “Sales Analytics” Screen (Sales Summary)

    This screen shows a summary of the entrepreneur’s sales performance. It displays key information such as total sales value, number of orders received, and a graphical sales trend. Time filters allow users to analyze sales data for different periods, including today, this month, or all time.


6. “Sales Analytics” Screen (Detailed Analysis)

    This screen provides detailed sales analytics, including top-selling products and a list of recent orders. The visual representation of data helps entrepreneurs identify high-performing products and monitor customer purchase activity more effectively.


7. “Order Details” Screen

    This screen displays detailed information for a selected customer order. It includes the order status, order timeline, customer information, ordered products, and total payment amount. The order progress is displayed step by step, allowing entrepreneurs to track the order from pending to completion.


8. “Entrepreneur Profile” Screen (Light Mode)

    This screen presents the entrepreneur’s profile information, including name, email address, phone number, business location, product categories, and a brief description. The social media icons shown on this screen are clickable and allow users to directly open the entrepreneur’s respective social media platforms, facilitating easier communication and online engagement. Users can also update their profile or change their password using the available options.


9. “Entrepreneur Profile” Screen (Dark Mode)

    This screen demonstrates the dark mode feature of the application. Dark mode provides a visually comfortable interface in low-light environments and allows users to switch between light and dark themes based on personal preference.


10. Business Location Map View

    This screen displays the entrepreneur’s business location using an interactive map. When the user clicks on the location information from the profile screen, the application opens the map view automatically. This feature allows customers to view and navigate to the farm or shop location easily, improving accessibility and transparency.


11. “Pineapple Products” Screen (Customer)

    This screen displays a list of pineapple products available for customers. Each product card includes an image, price, category, and stock status. Search and category filter functions help customers browse products efficiently, while the add-to-cart button allows products to be added directly to the shopping cart.



12. Product Details Screen (Customer View – Overview)

    This screen displays detailed information about a selected product, including product image, price, category, and available stock quantity. It also shows the entrepreneur selling the product. Customers can click the “Add to Cart” button to include the product in their shopping cart.


13. Product Details Screen (Customer View – Extended Information)

    This screen provides additional product details such as sales location and entrepreneur contact options. When the location is clicked, the application opens the map view to display the selling location. The social media icons are clickable and allow customers to contact the entrepreneur directly through external platforms.


14. “Announcements & Technical Information” Screen

    This screen displays general announcements and informational updates accessible to users. Each announcement includes a title, brief description, and validity period. This module helps keep both customers and entrepreneurs informed about promotions, availability, and important updates related to pineapple products.


15. Shopping Cart Screen

    This screen displays the shopping cart where selected products are listed before checkout. Customers can view product names, prices, and quantities. The quantity of each item can be increased or decreased, and items can be removed from the cart. The total payment amount is automatically calculated, and the “Proceed to Payment” button allows users to continue to the checkout process.


16. Payment Screen

    This screen shows the payment and checkout process. It includes an order summary displaying selected products, subtotal, delivery charges, and total payment amount. Customers are required to enter recipient details such as name and phone number. The screen also provides payment method options, including cash on delivery (COD), allowing users to confirm their order before submission.


17. Pineapple Entrepreneurs List Screen

    This screen displays a list of registered pineapple entrepreneurs available in the system. Customers can search entrepreneurs by name, location, or product category. Each entrepreneur card shows basic information such as business name, location, and product categories, enabling customers to choose an entrepreneur easily.


18. Entrepreneur Details Screen (Customer View)

    This screen shows detailed information about a selected entrepreneur. It includes contact details, business location, product categories, and a list of products sold by the entrepreneur. The social media icons displayed are clickable and allow customers to open the entrepreneur’s social media pages for direct communication.


19. Customer Profile Screen

    This screen presents the customer’s profile information, including name, email address, contact number, and delivery address. Customers can update their profile details or change their password. A purchase summary section displays total spending, number of orders made, and average spending per order.


20. Customer Purchase History Screen

    This screen shows the customer’s purchase history in detail. Each order record includes the order number, date, items purchased, and total payment amount. Customers can expand each order to view detailed product information, helping them track past purchases easily.
